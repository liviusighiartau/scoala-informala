This blog is about few of the most important things in life and how humans should treat them.

Who am I?


My name is Liviu. I was born in a small city named Dej, but my family was established in a small village near the city so more precisely that's where I'm coming from. I'm 27 years old and as many of you guys, I've had a lot of dreams set for my life. Some of them have happened, but in the meantime others have vanished due to circumstancies which we cannot control. I'm a convinced familist, believer and as suggested in the name of the blog, I believe that life is all about enjoying it as much as possible by living in the right and by having the foundation of everything you do on love.


What I enjoy doing

I am passionate about sports, music and of course my work. I really enjoy watching footbal games and from time to time to attend to live games. My favourite team in Romania is Steaua Bucharest and from outside Romania I'm a big FC Barcelona fan. I also like to play football a lot, with my friends, with my work colleagues. I'm also a small adventurer, I like to go hiking on the mountains.
I really love to spend time in nature with my loved ones or with my friends. My passion for music is something I got when I was born. Both, my mom and dad used to play to different instruments, my father was even an orchestra conductor back in the days. Some serious stuff

When it comes to work I quide myself by some principles which I consider very important.

  • Respect others because by this you respect yourself.
  • Do your work with integrity and honesty in order to earn respect.
  • Help others because by this you help yourself.
  • Always be transparent.


These principles help me not only work related but in every other activity in which I am involved. In the end the details make the difference.
